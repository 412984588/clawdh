# 30-Minute Recipe Collection

Every recipe here is tested to take 30 minutes or less from start to eating. No exceptions.

## Quick Cooking Rules

1. **Read the full recipe first** — Prevents mistakes and surprises
2. **Prep while cooking** — Chop veggies while water boils or meat sears
3. **Use a timer** — Don't guess; set your phone
4. **Hot pan, then food** — Preheat pans for better searing and faster cooking
5. **Mise en place** — Gather all ingredients before you start

## 30 Recipes in 30 Minutes

### Quick Proteins (10-Minute Mains)

**1. Pan-Seared Chicken Breast (12 min)**
```
Ingredients: 2 chicken breasts, salt, pepper, olive oil, butter, garlic
Method: Pound thin (even thickness). Season. Hot skillet + oil, 5 min per
side. Add butter + garlic last 2 min, baste.
Pairs with: Any side below
```

**2. Garlic Shrimp (8 min)**
```
Ingredients: 1 lb shrimp (peeled), 4 cloves garlic, butter, lemon, parsley
Method: Hot pan + butter. Cook shrimp 2 min per side. Add garlic 30 sec.
Squeeze lemon, top with parsley.
Pairs with: Rice, pasta, bread
```

**3. Crispy Tofu (15 min)**
```
Ingredients: 1 block firm tofu, cornstarch, soy sauce, sesame oil
Method: Press, cube. Toss in cornstarch. Pan-fry in oil until golden (8 min).
Toss with soy sauce + sesame oil.
Pairs with: Rice, noodles, stir-fry veggies
```

### One-Pan Meals (Full Dinners)

**4. Chicken Caesar Wraps (10 min)**
```
Ingredients: Rotisserie chicken, romaine, Caesar dressing, parmesan, tortillas
Method: Shred chicken. Chop romaine. Toss with dressing + parm. Wrap in
tortilla. Done.
```

**5. Stir-Fry (Any Protein + Veggies) (20 min)**
```
Ingredients: Protein (chicken/shrimp/tofu), mixed veggies, soy sauce,
garlic, ginger, rice (instant or leftover)
Method: Cook protein 5 min, set aside. Cook veggies 5 min. Combine with
sauce (soy + garlic + ginger + splash of water). Serve over rice.
```

**6. Sheet Pan Sausage + Veggies (25 min)**
```
Ingredients: Sausage links, bell peppers, onion, zucchini, olive oil, Italian seasoning
Method: Slice everything. Toss with oil + seasoning on sheet pan.
Broil at 425°F for 20 min. No stirring needed.
```

**7. Pasta Aglio e Olio (15 min)**
```
Ingredients: Spaghetti, 6 cloves garlic (sliced), olive oil, red pepper
flakes, parsley, parmesan
Method: Cook pasta. Meanwhile, heat oil + garlic + pepper flakes until
golden. Toss pasta with garlic oil + pasta water. Top with parsley + parm.
```

**8. Black Bean Tacos (15 min)**
```
Ingredients: Canned black beans, cumin, chili powder, tortillas, avocado,
salsa, sour cream, lime
Method: Heat beans with cumin + chili powder. Warm tortillas. Assemble with
toppings.
```

### Soups & Bowls (Warming)

**9. Egg Drop Soup (10 min)**
```
Ingredients: 4 cups chicken broth, 3 eggs, green onion, sesame oil,
cornstarch, soy sauce
Method: Boil broth. Whisk eggs. Slowly drizzle eggs into simmering broth
while stirring. Add soy + sesame oil. Top with green onion.
```

**10. Grain Bowl (15 min)**
```
Ingredients: Leftover rice/quinoa, canned chickpeas, cucumber, tomato,
feta, olive oil, lemon
Method: Warm grain. Layer with chickpeas + chopped veggies. Drizzle oil +
lemon. Crumble feta on top.
```

### Quick Breakfasts

**11. Shakshuka (20 min)**
```
Ingredients: Canned tomatoes, eggs, onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, bread
Method: Sauté onion + garlic. Add tomatoes + spices, simmer 10 min. Make
wells, crack in eggs. Cover and cook 5 min until set. Serve with bread.
```

**12. Veggie Omelette (10 min)**
```
Ingredients: 3 eggs, bell pepper, mushrooms, spinach, cheese
Method: Beat eggs. Sauté veggies 3 min. Pour eggs over, cook 2 min.
Add cheese, fold, cook 1 min more.
```
