# Pantry & Freezer Inventory

Know what you have. Stop buying duplicates. Reduce food waste.

## How to Use This Inventory

1. **Initial setup:** Walk through your pantry, fridge, and freezer. Log everything
2. **Weekly update:** After grocery shopping, add new items. After cooking, remove used items
3. **Before shopping:** Check this list to avoid rebuying what you have
4. **Monthly audit:** Check expiration dates, move oldest items to front

## Monthly Audit Checklist

- [ ] Check all expiration dates in pantry
- [ ] Check freezer — anything older than 3 months?
- [ ] Move oldest items to front (FIFO: first in, first out)
- [ ] Identify items to use this week before they expire
- [ ] Update restock flags
- [ ] Plan meals around what needs to be used up
- [ ] Generate grocery list from restock items
