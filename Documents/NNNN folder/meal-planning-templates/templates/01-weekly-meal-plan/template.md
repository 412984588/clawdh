## Dinner Rotation System

Stop reinventing meals every week. Assign themes to days:

| Day | Theme | Example Recipes |
|-----|-------|----------------|
| Monday | Meatless Monday | Veggie stir-fry, pasta primavera, bean tacos |
| Tuesday | Taco Tuesday | Chicken tacos, fish tacos, taco salad, burrito bowls |
| Wednesday | One-Pot Wednesday | Soups, stews, casseroles, curry |
| Thursday | Sheet Pan Thursday | Sheet pan chicken + veggies, salmon + asparagus |
| Friday | Pizza/Fun Friday | Homemade pizza, takeout, burgers |
| Saturday | Try Something New | New recipe from cookbook or online |
| Sunday | Slow Cooker Sunday | Pot roast, pulled pork, chili (makes leftovers) |

## Meal Planning Steps (15-Minute Process)

1. **Check calendar** (2 min) — Busy nights = simple meals or leftovers
2. **Check fridge/pantry** (3 min) — Use what you have first
3. **Pick 5 dinners** (3 min) — Use the rotation system above
4. **Plan breakfasts + lunches** (3 min) — Keep these simple and repeatable
5. **Write grocery list** (4 min) — See Grocery List template (02)

**Pro tip:** Plan on Saturday or Sunday. Shop once. Cook Sunday prep.
