## Picky Eater Strategies

| Strategy | How It Works | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Build-your-own | Let kids assemble their own meal | Taco bar, pizza, bento box |
| Hidden veggies | Blend vegetables into sauces | Spinach in smoothies, carrots in tomato sauce |
| The "try bite" rule | One bite of everything — no pressure to finish | Applies to new foods only |
| Familiar + new | Pair something new with something they love | New veggie + mac and cheese |
| Involve them | Kids eat food they helped prepare | Let them stir, pour, or assemble |
| No short-order cooking | Everyone eats the same dinner | Offer bread/fruit as safe fallback |
