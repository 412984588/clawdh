## Money-Saving Shopping Rules

1. **Check the pantry before shopping** — Don't rebuy what you already have
2. **Shop the perimeter first** — Fresh food around edges, processed in center
3. **Buy in season** — Produce in season costs 30–50% less
4. **Buy store brand staples** — Same quality, 20–40% cheaper for pantry items
5. **Buy protein in bulk** — Freeze what you won't use within 2 days
6. **Stick to the list** — Impulse buys add 20–30% to grocery bills
7. **Price per unit** — Compare by price per ounce/gram, not package price
