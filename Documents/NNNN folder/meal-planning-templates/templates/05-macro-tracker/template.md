# Macro & Nutrition Tracker

Track calories, protein, carbs, and fat to hit your nutrition goals — without obsessing over every gram.

## Your Daily Targets

**Goal:** ☐ Lose weight ☐ Maintain ☐ Gain muscle

| Macro | Daily Target | % of Calories | Notes |
|-------|-------------|--------------|-------|
| Calories | kcal | 100% | |
| Protein | g | % | 0.7–1g per lb body weight |
| Carbs | g | % | |
| Fat | g | % | Minimum 0.3g per lb body weight |
| Fiber | g | — | 25–35g recommended |
| Water | L | — | 2–3L recommended |

**How to calculate your targets:**
```
Maintenance calories = Body weight (lbs) × 14–16
Weight loss = Maintenance - 500 kcal/day (≈1 lb/week loss)
Muscle gain = Maintenance + 250–500 kcal/day
```
