## Budget Meal Formula

Every cheap meal follows this formula:

```
Cheap protein ($0.30–1.50/serving)
+ Bulk carb ($0.10–0.30/serving)
+ Seasonal vegetable ($0.30–0.75/serving)
+ Pantry seasoning ($0.05–0.15/serving)
= Complete meal for $0.75–2.70/serving
```

## Budget Shopping Rules

1. **Buy store brand** — Same manufacturer, 20–40% cheaper
2. **Buy in bulk when on sale** — Freeze meat, stock pantry staples
3. **Buy whole, not pre-cut** — Whole chicken < chicken breasts; whole carrots < baby carrots
4. **Buy frozen vegetables** — Same nutrition, longer shelf life, often cheaper
5. **Cook from scratch** — Pre-made meals cost 3–5× more than ingredients
6. **Eat leftovers** — Plan for meals that make 6+ servings
7. **Buy seasonal produce** — In-season = 30–50% cheaper
8. **Never shop hungry** — Impulse buys add 20–30% to your bill
