# FAQ — Meal Planning Templates

**Are these actual recipes or just planning templates?**
Both. The planning templates (weekly plan, grocery list, prep guide) are fill-in systems. The recipe templates (budget meals, 30-min recipes, family meals, freezer meals) include real recipes with ingredients, methods, and cost estimates.

**What format are the files?**
Markdown (.md) — open in Notion, Obsidian, Bear, Google Docs, or any text editor.

**Do I need cooking experience?**
No. The recipes range from beginner (10 min, 5 ingredients) to intermediate. The Meal Prep Guide walks you through a Sunday batch cooking session step by step.

**Will this actually save me money?**
The Budget Meals template includes 20 dinners under $3/serving with a sample $50/week meal plan for 2 people. The Pantry Inventory helps eliminate duplicate purchases and food waste ($1,500/year average).

**Is this good for families with picky eaters?**
Yes — template 06 (Family Meals) includes 20 kid-approved recipes, picky eater strategies, and school lunch ideas.

**Can I customize the templates?**
Absolutely. They're plain text files — edit to match your dietary needs, family size, and preferences.
