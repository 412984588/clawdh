# Side Hustle Starter Kit — Product Listing

## Tagline
Everything you need to launch a freelance side hustle — contracts, invoices, pricing, 50 ideas, and a 90-day action plan.

## Description
Most side hustle advice is vague. This kit is specific. 10 fill-in-the-bracket templates covering every practical need from "what should I sell?" to "how do I get paid?"

### What's included:
- **50 income ideas** ranked by startup cost, time to first dollar, and monthly earning potential
- **One-page business plan** — the only planning template you need before starting
- **Freelance contract** — covers scope, revisions, IP ownership, payment, and termination
- **Invoice template** — professional invoice + follow-up email sequence for late payments
- **Rate calculator** — exact formula to price your services + market rate benchmarks by industry
- **30-day content calendar** — social media plan with fill-in-the-blank captions for LinkedIn/Instagram
- **10 email templates** — cold outreach, proposals, follow-ups, testimonial requests
- **Finance tracker** — monthly income log, expense categories, and tax reserve calculator
- **Client onboarding system** — checklist, welcome email, kickoff call agenda, CRM tracker
- **90-day launch plan** — daily task list, weekly rhythm, progress tracker, when-to-pivot guide

## Tiers
- **Starter** ($19) — Templates 01–05: Idea to first proposal
- **Pro** ($39) — All 10 templates: Full operating system for your side hustle

## Format
Markdown (.md) files — open in Notion, Obsidian, Google Docs, or any text editor

## Who This Is For
Anyone who wants to earn extra income freelancing — writers, designers, consultants, virtual assistants, coaches, or any skilled professional who wants to monetize their expertise.
