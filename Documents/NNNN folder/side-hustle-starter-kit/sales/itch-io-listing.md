# itch.io Listing — Side Hustle Starter Kit

## Title
Side Hustle Starter Kit

## Blurb (280 chars)
10 practical templates to launch a freelance side hustle: 50 income ideas, business plan, contract, invoice, rate calculator, content calendar, email swipe file, finance tracker, client onboarding, 90-day plan.

## Description

**You don't need a course. You need templates.**

Most side hustle content teaches you *what* to do. This kit gives you the actual *documents* to do it.

---

### Starter — $19

**01 — 50 Income Ideas**
A ranked table of 50 proven side hustles with startup cost, time to first dollar, monthly potential, required skills, and difficulty. Highlighted picks for "no skills yet," "high hourly rate," and "passive income."

**02 — One-Page Business Plan**
Ten-section canvas: problem, solution, target customer, revenue model, marketing channels, 90-day goals, resources, competitive advantage, risks, and next 3 actions.

**03 — Freelance Contract**
Real contract covering: scope of work, timeline, payment schedule, revision policy, IP ownership, confidentiality, independent contractor status, termination clauses, and liability limits.

**04 — Invoice Template**
Professional invoice with numbering system, payment instructions, tracking log, and a 5-step follow-up sequence for late payments (including the exact email to send).

**05 — Rate Calculator**
Five-step process: income target → billable hours → minimum rate → market adjustment → project-based pricing. Includes 2024 rate benchmarks for 10 freelance categories.

---

### Pro — $39

Everything in Starter, plus:

**06 — 30-Day Content Calendar**
Platform guide, 4-week content plan with post types and caption frameworks, 50-item content ideas bank, and the caption formula that converts followers to clients.

**07 — Email Swipe File**
10 proven emails: cold outreach, follow-up, discovery call confirmation, proposal, invoice, project completion, testimonial request, re-engagement, price objection response, and polite decline.

**08 — Finance Tracker**
Monthly income log, expense log with categories, tax reserve calculator, quarterly summary, outstanding invoice tracker, income vs. goal comparison, and annual tax prep checklist.

**09 — Client Onboarding System**
Pre-project checklist, information to collect list, welcome email template, kickoff call agenda, mid-project check-in template, feedback request template, and client CRM tracker.

**10 — 90-Day Launch Plan**
Daily tasks for 90 days, weekly rhythm guide (15 hrs/week), progress tracker by week, and a when-to-pivot decision table.
