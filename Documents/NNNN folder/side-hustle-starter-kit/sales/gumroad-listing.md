# Gumroad Listing — Side Hustle Starter Kit

## Title
Side Hustle Starter Kit — 10 Templates to Go from Idea to First Paying Client

## Summary
Contracts, invoices, rate calculator, content calendar, 50 income ideas + a 90-day action plan. Everything to launch and run a freelance side hustle.

## Description

Most people want to start a side hustle. Most never do — because they don't know where to start, they don't have the paperwork ready, or they're afraid to charge money.

This kit removes every excuse.

**10 practical templates. Fill in the brackets. Go.**

---

**Starter ($19) — Templates 01-05: From Idea to First Proposal**

- **01 Income Ideas** — 50 proven side hustles with startup cost, time to first $, monthly potential, and required skills. Find your idea in 10 minutes.
- **02 Business Plan** — One-page canvas with sections for your problem, solution, target client, pricing, and 3 next actions. Write it in 30 minutes.
- **03 Contract Template** — Real freelance contract covering scope, payment schedule, revisions, IP ownership, and what happens if things go wrong.
- **04 Invoice Template** — Professional invoice + numbering system + follow-up email sequence for clients who don't pay on time.
- **05 Rate Calculator** — Step-by-step formula: income goal → billable hours → minimum rate → market-adjusted rate → project prices. Includes 2024 benchmarks for 10 industries.

---

**Pro ($39) — All 10 Templates: Full Operating System**

Everything above, plus:

- **06 Content Calendar** — 30-day social media plan with daily post types, caption formulas, and a 50-idea content bank for LinkedIn/Instagram
- **07 Email Templates** — 10 proven emails: cold outreach, follow-up, discovery call confirmation, proposal, invoice, project delivery, testimonial request, re-engagement, objection handling, and saying no gracefully
- **08 Finance Tracker** — Monthly income log, expense categories, tax reserve calculator (set aside 25-30% automatically), quarterly summary, outstanding invoice tracker
- **09 Client Onboarding** — Pre-project checklist, welcome email template, kickoff call agenda, feedback request template, offboarding checklist, client CRM
- **10 90-Day Launch Plan** — Daily task list for 90 days, weekly rhythm (15 hrs/week), progress tracker, and when-to-pivot decision guide
