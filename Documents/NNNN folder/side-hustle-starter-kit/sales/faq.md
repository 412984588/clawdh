# FAQ — Side Hustle Starter Kit

## Do I need any experience to use this?

No. The Income Ideas template (01) helps you pick a hustle that matches your current skills. The Business Plan and Rate Calculator walk you through everything step by step. If you've never freelanced before, start with Template 01, then 02, then 05.

## What kind of side hustles does this work for?

Any service-based freelance work: writing, design, consulting, virtual assistance, coaching, bookkeeping, social media management, web design, photography, video editing, tutoring, and more.

It also applies to productized services (selling a fixed-scope service at a fixed price) and digital products (if you combine it with a content/marketing strategy).

## What format are the files in?

Markdown (.md) files. They open in:
- **Notion** — paste content directly into a page
- **Obsidian** — add to your vault
- **Google Docs** — File → Open → upload .md file
- **Any text editor** — VS Code, Typora, iA Writer, etc.
- **Even basic text apps** — the formatting is clean plain text

## Can I edit and customize the templates?

Yes — that's the whole point. Fill in the brackets, add your rates, adjust the contract to match your services. The MIT license means you can modify and use everything for personal or commercial work.

## What's the difference between Starter and Pro?

**Starter ($19)** focuses on getting started: finding your idea, planning your business, setting rates, and having the legal/billing documents ready.

**Pro ($39)** adds the operational layer: how to attract clients through social media and email (templates 06–07), how to manage money and taxes (08), how to onboard clients smoothly (09), and a day-by-day 90-day launch plan (10).

If you're brand new, start with Starter. If you want the full system, get Pro.

## Is the contract template legally binding?

When signed by both parties, yes — but this is a starting template, not a substitute for a lawyer. For high-value contracts ($10,000+) or legally complex situations (NDAs, international clients, IP-heavy work), consult a lawyer. For typical freelance projects ($500–$5,000), this contract is solid.

## How long until I make my first sale/client?

The 90-Day Launch Plan (template 10) has people landing their first client within 30 days when they follow it consistently. Your result depends on your niche, your outreach volume, and whether you're in demand. But the templates remove every process obstacle — the only remaining variable is you doing the work.

## Can I resell or share these templates?

The MIT license allows personal and commercial use, but **reselling the templates themselves as a standalone product is not permitted**. You can use them in client work, include them as bonuses in your own paid products (with modification), or use them in your business.
