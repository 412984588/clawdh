# 30-Day Social Media Content Calendar

A plug-and-play content calendar for freelancers and side hustlers to build an audience and attract clients.

## How to Use

1. Choose your 1–2 primary platforms (pick based on where your clients are)
2. Fill in your niche/service in [BRACKETS]
3. Batch-create content on Sundays for the week ahead
4. Schedule using Buffer, Later, or Hootsuite

## Platform Guide

| Platform | Best for | Posting Frequency | Content type |
|----------|----------|------------------|--------------|
| LinkedIn | B2B services, consulting, coaching | 3–5x/week | Professional, long-form |
| Instagram | Creative services, lifestyle, products | 4–7x/week | Visual, stories, reels |
| Twitter/X | Writing, tech, commentary | 5–10x/week | Short, opinions, threads |
| TikTok | Education, entertainment, products | 1–3x/day | Short video |
| Facebook | Local services, groups, ads | 3–5x/week | Mixed |

## Content Ideas Bank (Never Run Out)

### Educational (40% of your content)
- "3 mistakes [your audience] makes with [problem]"
- "The [X]-step process I use for [task]"
- "How to [achieve goal] without [common obstacle]"
- "[Tool/platform] beginner guide"
- "Glossary: [industry terms explained simply]"

### Social Proof (20% of your content)
- Client testimonials (always get permission)
- Before/after results
- Project case studies
- Income/milestone updates
- DMs and messages (screenshot with permission)

### Behind the Scenes (20% of your content)
- Your workspace/setup
- Work in progress
- Day-in-the-life
- Tools you use
- Your creative process

### Personality (10% of your content)
- Why you started this side hustle
- Personal wins and failures
- Hot takes on your industry
- What you're learning
- Books/podcasts you recommend

### Offers (10% of your content)
- Service descriptions
- Package pricing
- Limited availability announcements
- Free resource downloads
- Discovery call invitations

## Caption Formula That Converts

```
HOOK (first line — stop the scroll)
→ "Most [freelancers/coaches/designers] make this mistake..."

BODY (deliver value)
→ [2–5 bullet points or short paragraphs]

BRIDGE (connect to you)
→ "This is exactly why I [your service/approach]..."

CTA (one clear action)
→ "Follow for more [topic] tips."
→ "DM me '[keyword]' to [get resource/book call]."
→ "Save this for later."
```
