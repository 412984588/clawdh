## Month 1: Foundation (Days 1–30)

### Goal: Set up your business infrastructure + land first client

---

### Week 1: Define & Position (Days 1–7)

**Daily time commitment: 1–2 hours**

| Day | Task | Done? |
|-----|------|-------|
| 1 | Choose your specific niche (not "designer" but "brand identity designer for wellness businesses") | [ ] |
| 2 | Research 10 competitors — what are they charging? What do they offer? | [ ] |
| 3 | Define your Ideal Client Profile (age, job, pain, goal, where they are online) | [ ] |
| 4 | Write your one-sentence value proposition: "I help [X] achieve [Y] by doing [Z]" | [ ] |
| 5 | Decide on 1 core service to sell first (keep it simple) | [ ] |
| 6 | Set your rates using the Rate Calculator (template 05) | [ ] |
| 7 | Rest and review. Does your niche + offer + rate make sense? | [ ] |

**Week 1 checkpoint:** Can you explain your service to a stranger in 1 sentence? If not, refine.

## Weekly Rhythm

Structure your weekly hours for consistency:

| Day | Activity | Hours |
|-----|----------|-------|
| Monday | Client work | 3 hrs |
| Tuesday | Client work + 10 outreach messages | 3 hrs |
| Wednesday | Content creation (1 post) + client work | 2 hrs |
| Thursday | Client work + follow-ups | 3 hrs |
| Friday | Admin (invoices, emails) + 10 outreach | 2 hrs |
| Saturday | Optional: batch next week's content | 1 hr |
| Sunday | Review week, plan next week | 30 min |

**Total: ~15 hrs/week**

## When to Pivot

Signs you should change your approach (not quit):

| Sign | Action |
|------|--------|
| 50+ outreach messages, 0 responses | Rewrite your pitch. Change the hook. |
| Getting calls but no clients | Your proposal or price is off. Lower price or add value. |
| Getting clients but not profitable | Your rates are too low. Calculate using template 05. |
| Burning out on the hustle | Narrow to 1 service. Raise prices. Work less. |
