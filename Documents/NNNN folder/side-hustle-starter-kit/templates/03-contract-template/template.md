## 5. Intellectual Property

**Upon receipt of full payment**, Client owns all rights to the final deliverables.

**Until full payment is received:** Provider retains all rights to the work.

**Portfolio rights:** Provider may display the work in their portfolio and marketing materials unless Client requests otherwise in writing.

**Third-party assets:** Client is responsible for obtaining licenses for any stock photos, fonts, or other third-party assets they provide. Provider will notify Client of any licensed assets used.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information shared during this project. This includes but is not limited to business strategies, client lists, financial information, and unreleased products.

This obligation continues for **[2] years** after the contract ends.

## 7. Independent Contractor

Provider is an independent contractor, not an employee of Client. Provider is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and business expenses.

---

## 8. Termination

**By Client:** Client may terminate this agreement with [7] days written notice. Client owes payment for all work completed to date, plus the non-refundable deposit.

**By Provider:** Provider may terminate with [7] days written notice if Client fails to pay, fails to provide needed materials after [14] days of request, or engages in abusive behavior. Provider will deliver all completed work upon receipt of payment for work completed.
