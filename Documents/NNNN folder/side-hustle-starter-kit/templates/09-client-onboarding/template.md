# Client Onboarding System

A smooth onboarding turns a new client into a loyal, long-term client.
Use this checklist and template system for every new project.

---

## Onboarding Checklist

### Before Day 1 (Before Work Begins)

- [ ] Contract signed by both parties
- [ ] Deposit received (confirm in bank)
- [ ] Project added to your project management tool
- [ ] Send welcome email (template below)
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call (if applicable)
- [ ] Create shared folder (Google Drive, Dropbox, or Notion)
- [ ] Add client contact info to your CRM/spreadsheet
- [ ] Set project deadline in your calendar with reminder 1 week before

### Kickoff Call Agenda (30–45 min)

- [ ] Introductions (5 min)
- [ ] Review project scope and deliverables (10 min)
- [ ] Confirm timeline and milestones (5 min)
- [ ] Discuss communication preferences: response time, preferred channel (5 min)
- [ ] Client provides required assets/information (10 min)
- [ ] Q&A and next steps (5 min)

## Feedback Request Template

*Send with every draft deliverable.*

**Subject:** [Project Name] — [Deliverable Name] ready for your review

> Hi [First Name],
>
> [Deliverable] is ready for your review:
>
> 📎 [Link to deliverable / attached file]
>
> **To give feedback, please:**
> 1. Review by [date — ideally 3–5 business days]
> 2. Use [comments / tracked changes / the form below] to share your thoughts
> 3. Consolidate all feedback into one response (not multiple emails)
>
> **Specific questions I'd love your input on:**
> - [Question about specific design choice or direction]
> - [Question about content or messaging]
>
> This is round [1 of 2] of included revisions.
>
> Let me know if you have any questions!
>
> [Your Name]

## Offboarding Checklist (Project Completion)

- [ ] All final files delivered
- [ ] Client confirms they received and can access everything
- [ ] Final invoice sent and paid
- [ ] Shared folder organized and access confirmed
- [ ] Testimonial requested (see email template 07)
- [ ] Project added to portfolio
- [ ] Follow-up scheduled in 90 days (see email template 08)
- [ ] Notes added to CRM about client preferences, communication style
