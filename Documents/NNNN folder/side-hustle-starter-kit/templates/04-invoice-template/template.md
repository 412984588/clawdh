## Invoice Numbering System

Use a consistent numbering system to stay organized:

```
Format: [CLIENT INITIALS]-[YEAR]-[NUMBER]
Examples:
  ABC-2024-001   (first invoice to ABC Corp in 2024)
  ABC-2024-002   (second invoice to ABC Corp)
  XYZ-2024-001   (first invoice to XYZ Ltd)
```

Or use sequential: `INV-001`, `INV-002`, etc.

## Follow-Up Schedule for Unpaid Invoices

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Due date | Check if paid |
| +3 days | Friendly reminder email |
| +7 days | Second reminder, polite but direct |
| +14 days | Phone call / formal notice |
| +30 days | Final notice, mention late fees |
| +45 days | Consider collections or small claims |
