# Side Hustle Starter Kit

Everything you need to launch a freelance side hustle from zero to first paying client — in one pack.

## What's Inside

| # | Template | What It Does |
|---|----------|--------------|
| 01 | Income Ideas | 50 proven side hustles with startup cost, timeline, and income potential |
| 02 | Business Plan | One-page canvas to define your offer, market, and 90-day goals |
| 03 | Contract Template | Freelance contract covering scope, payment, IP, revisions, termination |
| 04 | Invoice Template | Professional invoice with numbering system and follow-up schedule |
| 05 | Rate Calculator | Step-by-step formula to calculate your freelance rate + market benchmarks |
| 06 | Content Calendar | 30-day social media plan to attract clients on LinkedIn/Instagram |
| 07 | Email Templates | 10 proven emails: cold outreach, proposals, invoices, testimonial requests |
| 08 | Finance Tracker | Monthly income/expense log, tax calculator, quarterly summary |
| 09 | Client Onboarding | Onboarding checklist, welcome email, kickoff agenda, CRM tracker |
| 10 | 90-Day Launch Plan | Week-by-week action plan to go from zero to first client |

## Tiers

- **Starter** ($19) — Templates 01–05: From idea to your first proposal
- **Pro** ($39) — All 10 templates: Full operating system for your side hustle

## Quick Start

**Day 1:** Open `01-income-ideas/template.md` → pick your hustle
**Day 2:** Fill in `02-business-plan/template.md` → know your offer and target
**Day 3:** Customize `03-contract-template/template.md` and `04-invoice-template/template.md`
**Day 4:** Use `05-rate-calculator/template.md` → set your prices
**Day 5:** Start outreach using `07-email-templates/template.md`

## File Formats

All templates are `.md` (Markdown) files — open in any text editor, Notion, Obsidian, or Google Docs.
