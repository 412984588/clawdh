# FAQ — Notion Agent Cost Optimizer

**What AI providers does this cover?**
The pricing tables cover OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini, o1, o3-mini), Anthropic (Claude Opus 4, Sonnet 4, Haiku 3.5), and Google (Gemini 2.5 Pro/Flash). The tracking templates work with any provider — just add your models.

**Do I need to be technical to use this?**
No. These are fill-in-the-blank tracking templates, not code. If you can use a spreadsheet, you can use these.

**What format are the files?**
Markdown (.md) — paste directly into Notion, import into Obsidian, or open in any text editor.

**Is the pricing data up to date?**
Pricing reflects 2024 rates. AI model prices change frequently — the templates include links to official pricing pages so you can verify and update.

**What's the difference between Starter and Pro?**
Starter gives you the 5 core tracking and planning templates. Pro adds prompt optimization techniques, vendor comparison, alerting playbooks, team allocation, and executive reporting.

**Can I use this for a team?**
Yes. Template 09 (Team Allocation) is designed specifically for multi-team organizations. The license covers personal and small team use (up to 5 users).

**How much can I actually save?**
Most teams find 20–40% cost reduction from model selection alone (using cheaper models for simple tasks). Prompt optimization can save an additional 30–70% on individual calls. The Prompt Optimizer template includes before/after examples.
