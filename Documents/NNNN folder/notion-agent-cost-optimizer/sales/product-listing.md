# Notion Agent Cost Optimizer

**Know exactly where every AI dollar goes. Cut waste. Scale smart.**

## The Problem

AI API bills are the new cloud bills — they start small, grow fast, and nobody knows where the money goes until it's too late.

Teams spinning up GPT-4o agents, Claude workflows, and Gemini pipelines have no visibility into per-agent costs, no budgets, and no optimization framework. The result: surprise bills, wasted tokens, and no way to prove ROI.
