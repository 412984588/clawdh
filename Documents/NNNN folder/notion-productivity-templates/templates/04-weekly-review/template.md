# Weekly Review Template — Notion

A 20-minute Sunday ritual to close the week, capture learnings, and plan with intention.

---

## How to Use

1. Block 20 minutes every Sunday (or your week's last day)
2. Open this template in a new page for each week
3. Duplicate page and rename: "Week of [Date]"
4. Fill it out before planning the next week

## Quarterly Pattern Review

Every 13 weeks, review your archive and answer:

- What was my average energy score?
- What was my most common "win" category?
- What recurring challenges keep showing up?
- What habit has been most consistent?
- What needs to change next quarter?

---

## Tips for Better Weekly Reviews

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Same time, same place every week | Reduces friction |
| Do it before Monday planning | Close the past before opening the future |
| Be honest, not harsh | Self-criticism kills the habit |
| Keep old reviews | Patterns become clear over months |
| Short answers are fine | Done > perfect |
