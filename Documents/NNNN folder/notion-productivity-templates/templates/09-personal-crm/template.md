## Re-Engagement Templates

### Reconnecting After a Long Time

> **Subject:** Hey [Name] — thinking of you

> Hi [Name],
>
> It's been a while! I was [reminder of how you're connected — e.g., "looking at old photos from our time at X"] and thought of you.
>
> How are things going with [something specific you remember about them]?
>
> I'd love to catch up. Are you open to a quick call sometime in the next few weeks?
>
> Best,
> [Your name]

---

### Congratulating on Achievement

> Hi [Name],
>
> I just saw [LinkedIn / announcement / news] — congratulations on [achievement]!
>
> That's a big deal. I'd love to hear more about how it's going when you have a moment.
>
> Congrats again!
>
> [Your name]

---

### Sending a Resource

> Hi [Name],
>
> I came across [article/book/tool] and immediately thought of you because [reason — relates to something they care about].
>
> [Link or attachment]
>
> Hope it's useful!
>
> [Your name]

## Relationship Tiers

| Tier | Who | How often to reach out | How |
|------|-----|----------------------|-----|
| 🌟 Inner circle | 5–10 people (closest) | Weekly / Monthly | Any way |
| 🤝 Active network | 20–50 people | Quarterly | Email, coffee, events |
| 📡 Extended network | 50–200 people | 1–2x per year | LinkedIn, email |
| ❄️ Dormant | Everyone else | When relevant | Reactive |
