## Time Blocking Guide

| Block Type | Length | When | What |
|-----------|--------|------|------|
| Deep Work | 90–120 min | Morning (peak energy) | Complex tasks, creative work, writing |
| Shallow Work | 30–60 min | Afternoon | Email, admin, quick tasks |
| Meeting Block | Variable | Schedule together | Batch meetings into 1–2 time windows |
| Buffer | 30 min | Between blocks | Overflow, unexpected items |
| Shutdown Ritual | 15 min | End of workday | Clear desk, set tomorrow, close tabs |

---

## Energy Management

Plan your day based on YOUR energy pattern (most people peak in the morning):

```
High energy ████████░░░░░░░░
             6am    9am   noon  3pm   6pm

🔴 Do your hardest tasks during peak energy
🟡 Do meetings and collaborative work during medium energy
🟢 Do admin, email, and passive tasks during low energy
```

## Task Prioritization: Eisenhower Matrix

| | Urgent | Not Urgent |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **Important** | 🔴 Do now (MIT) | 🟡 Schedule |
| **Not Important** | 🟠 Delegate | 🟢 Delete |

**Rule:** Most people spend too much time in "urgent/not important" (other people's requests).
**Goal:** Spend more time in "important/not urgent" (your actual goals).

## Shutdown Ritual (5 min)

Run this every evening before closing Notion:

- [ ] Capture any open loops (incomplete tasks, things on my mind)
- [ ] Check tomorrow's calendar
- [ ] Set tomorrow's MIT
- [ ] Update habit tracker
- [ ] Close all browser tabs related to work
- [ ] Say out loud: "Work is done for today."
