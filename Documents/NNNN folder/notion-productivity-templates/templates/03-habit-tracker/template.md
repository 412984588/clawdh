# Habit Tracker — Notion Template

Build lasting habits with daily tracking, streak counters, and monthly progress views.
Based on research from "Atomic Habits" — habits stacked on identity and environment design.

## Habit Design Worksheet

For each new habit, fill this out before you start:

```
## Habit: [Name]

### The 4-Step Habit Loop (Atomic Habits)
1. **Cue (Make it obvious):** [What will remind you to do this?]
   Example: "After I pour my morning coffee..."

2. **Craving (Make it attractive):** [Why do you actually want to do this?]
   Example: "...I will exercise, because I want to feel energized all day"

3. **Routine:** [The actual habit — be specific about duration/quantity]
   Example: "20-minute run, minimum 2km"

4. **Reward (Make it satisfying):** [Immediate reward after completing]
   Example: "Track my streak in Notion + make a nice breakfast"

### Implementation Intention
"I will [HABIT] at [TIME] in [LOCATION]."
> [e.g., "I will meditate for 10 minutes at 7:00am at my desk."]

### Habit Stacking
Attach to an existing habit: "After I [EXISTING HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT]."
> [e.g., "After I brew my coffee, I will write 3 things I'm grateful for."]

### Reducing Friction
What makes this habit harder? How will you remove the obstacle?
| Obstacle | Solution |
|----------|---------|
| [e.g., Gym bag not ready] | [Pack it the night before] |
| [e.g., App distracting] | [Delete from phone, use desktop only] |

### Identity Statement
"I am the type of person who [identity this habit reinforces]."
> [e.g., "I am the type of person who moves their body every day."]
```

## Streak Milestones

Celebrate progress at these checkpoints:

| Days | Milestone | What to do |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| 7 | One week 🌱 | Note how you feel |
| 21 | Three weeks 🔥 | Share with someone |
| 30 | One month 💪 | Review and adjust difficulty |
| 66 | Two months 🧠 | Habit is now automatic — consider leveling up |
| 100 | Century ⭐ | Major celebration |
| 365 | One year 🏆 | Part of your identity |
