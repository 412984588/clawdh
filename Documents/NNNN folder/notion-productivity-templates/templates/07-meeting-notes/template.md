## Meeting Best Practices

| Practice | Why It Matters |
|----------|---------------|
| Send agenda 24hrs before | Everyone comes prepared |
| Start with purpose statement | Keeps meeting on track |
| Assign action items in meeting | Don't wait until after |
| End 5 min early | Leave time to capture notes |
| Send summary within 1 hour | While it's fresh; creates accountability |
| Track action item completion | Follow-through is everything |

## Meeting Audit (Monthly)

Ask these questions about your meetings:

- Which recurring meetings could be eliminated?
- Which meetings could be shorter?
- Which could be an async update instead?
- Am I the right person for each meeting?
