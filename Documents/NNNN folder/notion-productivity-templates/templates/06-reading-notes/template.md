## Note-Taking System

### When to Highlight / Note
| Situation | What to Do |
|-----------|-----------|
| Something surprises you | Highlight + note "Surprised" |
| An idea you want to use | Highlight + note "Apply" |
| Disagree with author | Note "Disagree" + your counter |
| Connects to another idea | Note "Link" + what it connects to |
| A quotable sentence | Double-highlight |

### Transfer to Notion Rules
- Process notes within 48 hours of finishing
- Rewrite in your own words (don't just copy quotes)
- One insight per note = better recall
- Link to related notes and goals
