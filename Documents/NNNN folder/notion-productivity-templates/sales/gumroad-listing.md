# Gumroad Listing — Notion Productivity Templates Pack

## Title
Notion Productivity Templates Pack — 10 Connected Templates for Your Whole Life

## Summary
Projects, goals, habits, reviews, reading notes, meetings, second brain, CRM, and daily planner. All connected. Ready to paste into Notion.

## Description

Most Notion templates are beautiful but shallow — a pretty table with no system behind it.

This pack is different. Every template is designed to connect to the others:

- Your **Daily Planner** focuses on today's MIT (Most Important Task)
- Your **Weekly Review** checks progress on habits and goals
- Your **Monthly Review** scores your life areas and updates your annual goals
- Your **Goal Tracker** connects to your **Project Management** board
- Your **Reading Notes** feed into your **Second Brain**
- Your **Personal CRM** reminds you to follow up with people who matter

**It's a productivity operating system, not just templates.**

---

### Starter — $29 (Templates 01–05)

**01 — Project Management Kanban**
Full database schema with Status/Priority/Assignee/Due Date properties. Five views: Kanban by status, Kanban by priority, Timeline/Gantt, My Tasks, This Week. Project overview page, standup template, and prioritization matrix.

**02 — Goal Tracker**
OKR-based system with life area scoring, annual goals canvas, quarterly breakdowns, monthly check-ins, and goal grading scale. Never set a goal you forget about.

**03 — Habit Tracker**
Daily habit log, monthly habit grid (visual checkbox calendar), habit design worksheet using the 4-step habit loop, streak milestones, and monthly habit reflection.

**04 — Weekly Review**
20-minute Sunday ritual: wins, challenges, lessons, goal progress, habits score, relationships check, money check, energy review, and next week's top 3 priorities.

**05 — Monthly Review**
45-minute life audit: life area scoring (8 areas, 1–10), monthly wins, financial review, learning recap, relationship check, and next month's design.
