# Notion Productivity Templates Pack — Product Listing

## Tagline
10 Notion templates to run your projects, build habits, and track goals — all in one workspace.

## Description
Most Notion templates look pretty but don't work as a system. This pack is designed differently — every template connects to the others, so your daily plans feed your weekly reviews, your goals drive your projects, and your reading notes feed your second brain.

**Starter ($29) — Templates 01–05:**
- Project Management Kanban with full database schema and 5 views
- Goal Tracker using the OKR framework with quarterly breakdowns
- Habit Tracker with daily log, monthly grid, streak tracking, and habit design worksheet
- Weekly Review — 20-minute Sunday ritual
- Monthly Review — 45-minute life audit with life area scoring

**Pro ($49) — All 10 templates:**
Everything above, plus:
- Reading Notes System with progressive summarization method
- Meeting Notes — universal, 1:1, and client call formats
- Second Brain using PARA method (Projects/Areas/Resources/Archive)
- Personal CRM with contact profiles and follow-up tracker
- Daily Planner with MIT method, time blocking, and shutdown ritual

## Format
Markdown (.md) files — paste into Notion, import to Obsidian, or open in any text editor.

## Who This Is For
Notion users, knowledge workers, students, entrepreneurs, and anyone who wants a structured productivity system they'll actually stick with.
