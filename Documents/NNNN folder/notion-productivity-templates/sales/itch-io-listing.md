# itch.io Listing — Notion Productivity Templates Pack

## Title
Notion Productivity Templates Pack

## Blurb
10 connected Notion templates: project kanban, goal tracker, habit tracker, weekly/monthly reviews, reading notes, meeting notes, second brain, personal CRM, daily planner.

## Description

**Build the Notion workspace you've always wanted.**

Not just templates — a complete productivity system where every piece connects to the others.

---

### Starter — $29

**01 Project Management Kanban**
Database schema with 8 properties (Status, Priority, Assignee, Due Date). Five views: status board, priority board, timeline/Gantt, my tasks, this week. Project overview page template, weekly standup format, and prioritization matrix.

**02 Goal Tracker**
Annual goal canvas with life area scoring (8 areas, 1–10). OKR structure with 2–3 key results per goal. Quarterly breakdowns. Monthly check-in template. Goal grading scale (0–1.0). Goal archive.

**03 Habit Tracker**
Daily habit log with morning/work/evening sections. Monthly visual grid (30-day checkbox calendar). Habit design worksheet using the 4-step loop (Cue → Craving → Routine → Reward). Streak milestone guide. Monthly reflection.

**04 Weekly Review**
Two-part template: Part 1 reviews wins, challenges, goals, habits, relationships, money, and energy. Part 2 plans next week's top 3 priorities, calendar scan, and inbox clear. 20 minutes.

**05 Monthly Review**
Eight life areas scored 1–10. Monthly wins, financial review by category, learning recap, relationship check, goals review with progress tracking. Next month's design with theme, top 3 goals, habit plan, and intention statement.

### Pro — $49

**06 Reading Notes** — Book library database, progressive summarization note template, annual reading tracker, note-taking system for books/podcasts/articles

**07 Meeting Notes** — Three formats: universal, 1:1 (manager/report), and client calls. Agenda, decisions table, action items with owners and due dates, open questions, follow-up templates.

**08 Second Brain** — PARA structure setup (Projects/Areas/Resources/Archive), universal note template, progressive summarization method, capture habits guide, weekly maintenance checklist

**09 Personal CRM** — Contact profiles with relationship history, follow-up queue, networking event notes, re-engagement email templates, birthday tracker, relationship tiers guide

**10 Daily Planner** — MIT (Most Important Task) method, time blocking schedule template, energy management framework, Eisenhower matrix, weekly task inbox, shutdown ritual checklist
