# Wedding Master Checklist

Use this wedding checklist as the single source of truth from engagement to the
last reception cleanup task. Start with venue, ceremony date, and rough guest
count, then map each month to a concrete deliverable for the bride, groom, and
planner. Include dress fitting, suit selection, flower tasting, cake testing,
vendor deposits, RSVP deadlines, ceremony rehearsal, and reception setup. Keep
one owner per line item and a firm due date so no guest-facing promise slips.
