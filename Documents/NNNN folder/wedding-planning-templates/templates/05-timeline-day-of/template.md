# Wedding Day-Of Timeline

Build a precise ceremony and reception run-of-show with timestamps for hair and
makeup, photographer arrival, flower delivery, cake delivery, family portraits,
guest seating, ceremony start, cocktail hour, speeches, dinner service, first
dance, and final send-off. Include contact numbers for venue staff and every
vendor. A strong day-of timeline reduces wedding-day anxiety because the bride,
groom, and wedding party know exactly where to be, when the guest flow changes,
and who is responsible for solving late arrivals or setup issues.
