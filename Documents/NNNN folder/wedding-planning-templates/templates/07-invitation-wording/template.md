# Wedding Invitation Wording Guide

Templates for every situation — formal, casual, second marriage, destination, and digital.

## Classic Formal Wording

### Traditional (Bride's Parents Hosting)
```
Mr. and Mrs. [Bride's Parents' Names]
request the honour of your presence
at the marriage of their daughter

[Bride's Full Name]
to
[Groom's Full Name]

Saturday, the [date] of [month]
two thousand twenty-five
at [time]

[Venue Name]
[City, State]

Reception to follow
```

### Both Sets of Parents Hosting
```
Mr. and Mrs. [Bride's Parents]
and
Mr. and Mrs. [Groom's Parents]
request the honour of your presence
at the marriage of

[Bride's Name]
and
[Groom's Name]

[Date]
[Time]
[Venue]
[City, State]

Dinner and dancing to follow
```

### Couple Hosting
```
Together with their families

[Name]
and
[Name]

invite you to celebrate their marriage

[Date]
[Time]
[Venue]
[City, State]

Reception to follow
```

## Modern / Casual Wording

### Relaxed
```
[Name] and [Name]
are getting married!

Join us to celebrate

[Date] at [Time]
[Venue]
[City, State]

Dinner, drinks, and dancing to follow

RSVP by [date] at [website/email]
```

### Short and Sweet
```
We're tying the knot!

[Name] & [Name]

[Date] | [Time]
[Venue]

RSVP: [website]
```

---

## Special Situations

### Destination Wedding
```
[Name] and [Name]

invite you to join them in [destination]
as they celebrate their wedding

[Date] at [Time]
[Venue Name]
[City, Country]

Travel and accommodation details at [website]
```

### Rehearsal Dinner
```
Please join us for a rehearsal dinner
in honor of

[Bride] and [Groom]

[Date] at [Time]
[Restaurant/Venue]
[Address]

Hosted by [Groom's Parents' Names]
```

### Post-Wedding Brunch
```
Join us for a farewell brunch!

The morning after [Name] & [Name]'s wedding

[Date] at [Time]
[Location]

Casual dress — come hungry!
```

## RSVP Card Wording

### Formal
```
Kindly reply by [date]

M_________________
___ accepts with pleasure
___ declines with regret

Number attending: ___

Meal preference:
☐ Chicken   ☐ Beef   ☐ Vegetarian

Dietary restrictions: _______________
```

### Casual
```
RSVP by [date] at [website]

Will you be joining us?
☐ Wouldn't miss it!
☐ Sorry, can't make it

Name(s): ___
Meal: Chicken / Beef / Veggie
Song request: ___
```

## Addressing Etiquette

| Situation | Address As |
|-----------|----------|
| Married couple | Mr. and Mrs. [Husband's Full Name] |
| Unmarried couple | Ms. [Name] and Mr. [Name] |
| Family with children | The [Last Name] Family |
| Single with plus-one | [Name] and Guest |
| Doctor | Dr. [Name] |

## Invitation Timeline

| Task | When |
|------|------|
| Order invitations | 4–6 months before wedding |
| Mail invitations | 6–8 weeks before (8–12 for destination) |
| RSVP deadline | 3–4 weeks before wedding |
| Follow up on missing RSVPs | 1 week after deadline |
