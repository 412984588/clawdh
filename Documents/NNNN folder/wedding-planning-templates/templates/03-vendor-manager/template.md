# Wedding Vendor Manager

Manage venue, florist, cake artist, photographer, DJ, officiant, rentals, and
reception caterer in one comparison table. Capture quote date, contact name,
package summary, cancellation terms, payment schedule, and what each vendor is
expected to deliver on ceremony day. Add a “risk” field for late replies, vague
contracts, or hidden fees. This template makes it easier to compare flower and
food vendors side by side instead of relying on memory after a busy week of
sales calls and tasting appointments.
