## Booking Checklist

- [ ] Book flights (book 3–6 months in advance for best prices)
- [ ] Book accommodation
- [ ] Book airport transfers
- [ ] Book must-do activities / excursions
- [ ] Purchase travel insurance
- [ ] Check passport expiration dates
- [ ] Apply for visa (if needed — allow 4–8 weeks)
- [ ] Notify bank of travel dates (avoid card blocks)
- [ ] Set up international phone plan
- [ ] Print/save all confirmations
