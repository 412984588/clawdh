# Wedding Guest List Tracker

Track every guest from save-the-date through final reception headcount. The
template includes household grouping, plus-one rules, RSVP status, meal choice,
allergy notes, hotel needs, gift tracking, and ceremony seating constraints.
Flag sensitive family groupings early so the bride and groom can resolve them
before invitation printing. Keep one column for “must greet” so the couple does
not miss important guests during the reception. Update the sheet every time a
vendor asks for final numbers and lock the venue count before catering cutoffs.
