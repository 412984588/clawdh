# Wedding Budget Tracker

Track the full wedding budget by category: venue, ceremony, reception food,
flower design, cake, dress, suit, music, photography, stationery, and guest
transport. Use estimated, quoted, paid, and remaining columns to avoid losing
control after the first vendor deposits. Add a contingency line for rush fees
and last-minute guest count changes. Review this tracker weekly with the bride,
groom, or family contributors so decisions on upgrades stay grounded in the
real budget rather than emotional spending.
