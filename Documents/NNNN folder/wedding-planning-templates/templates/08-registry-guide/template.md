# Wedding Registry Guide

What to register for, where to register, and how much — so guests find gifts at every price point.

## Registry Checklist

- [ ] Choose 2–3 platforms (Amazon + Zola + one specialty store)
- [ ] Register for items across all price ranges ($15–$500+)
- [ ] Include 1.5× items per guest (150 guests = 225 items)
- [ ] Add a cash/honeymoon fund option
- [ ] Share registry link on wedding website

## Platform Comparison

| Platform | Cash Fund? | Completion Discount | Best For |

## Registry Tips for the Bride and Groom

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| 20–30 items under $50 | These get bought first — every guest budget covered |
| Register for upgrades | Replace what you have with the NICE version |
| Skip what you won't use | Breadmaker? Fondue set? Be honest |
| Include group gift options | Let guests combine for big items |
| Use completion discount | Buy remaining items at 10–20% off post-wedding |
