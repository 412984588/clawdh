## How to Use This Pack

1. **Choose your platforms** — focus on 1–2 platforms first
2. **Open the platform template** — follow the 30-day calendar
3. **Batch your content** — use template 08 to create a month of content in one day
4. **Track your results** — use template 09 weekly and monthly
5. **Plan your next quarter** — use template 10 to set strategy

## Quick Start (10 Minutes)

1. Open your primary platform template
2. Read the Platform Overview table
3. Copy the weekly calendar for Week 1
4. Use the caption formulas to write your first 3 posts
5. Schedule them in your tool of choice (Later, Buffer, Metricool)
