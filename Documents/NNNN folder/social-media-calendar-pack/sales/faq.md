# FAQ — Social Media Content Calendar Pack

## General

**What's the difference between Starter and Pro?**

Starter includes 5 platform-specific 30-day calendars (Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, TikTok, Facebook). Pro adds YouTube, Pinterest, the Content Batching System, Analytics Tracker, and the 90-Day Strategy — everything needed to plan, batch, execute, and measure a full content program.

**What format are the files?**

All templates are Markdown (.md) files. Open them in Notion (paste or import), Obsidian, Bear, Typora, Google Docs (import), or any plain text editor. No special software required.

**Do I need to be tech-savvy to use these?**

No. Markdown is plain text with simple formatting (# for headings, | for tables). If you can open a Word document, you can use these files.

**Can I customize the templates?**

Yes — they're yours to edit however you like. Swap in your niche, change the schedule, add or remove content types.

## Content

**These calendars say "your niche" a lot. Do I have to figure that out myself?**

Yes — the templates assume you know who your audience is and what topic you cover. If you haven't defined your niche, the 90-Day Strategy template (Pro) includes an audience definition worksheet to help.

**Are these good for beginners or advanced creators?**

Both. Beginners can follow the 30-day calendars as-is. Advanced creators will find the Analytics Tracker and 90-Day Strategy most useful for optimizing what they're already doing.

**Do the strategies work for local businesses, not just personal brands?**

Yes. The platforms covered (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) are the most relevant for local businesses. The calendar ideas are written to be niche-agnostic and work equally well for a bakery, a law firm, or a freelancer.

**Is this up to date for 2024?**

Yes. Platform notes, algorithm insights, and format recommendations (Reels, TikTok, Shorts, LinkedIn carousels) reflect 2024 best practices.

## Usage

**How do I use the Content Batching System?**

Open template 08 and follow the 8-hour Content Day schedule. You'll ideate (60 min), write captions (120 min), create visuals (105 min), record videos (120 min), edit (60 min), and schedule (60 min) — producing ~30 posts in one day.

**What scheduling tool should I use?**

The pack works with any scheduler. Popular options: Later (Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest), Buffer (most platforms), Metricool (all platforms including YouTube), Tailwind (Pinterest, Instagram).

**How many platforms should I focus on?**

The 90-Day Strategy template recommends starting with 1–2 platforms and doing them well. Most creators fail by spreading thin across 5 platforms. Pick where your audience already is.
