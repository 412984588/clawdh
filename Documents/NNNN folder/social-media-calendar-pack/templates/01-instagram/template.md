# Instagram Content Calendar

A complete 30-day Instagram strategy with post types, caption formulas, and scheduling guide.

## Platform Overview

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Best posting frequency | 4–7x/week feed + 5–10 stories/day | Consistency > volume |
| Peak posting times | Tue–Fri, 9–11am & 7–9pm (your timezone) | Test with your audience |
| Best content types | Reels (reach), Carousels (saves), Stories (engagement) | Reels get 3–5× more reach |
| Optimal caption length | 125–150 chars (above fold) | Front-load the hook |
| Hashtags | 3–5 targeted | Instagram deprioritized mass hashtags in 2024 |

---

## Content Mix (The 80/20 Rule)

| Content Type | % of Posts | Goal |
|-------------|-----------|------|
| Educational / Value | 40% | Build authority, get saves |
| Behind the scenes | 20% | Build trust and connection |
| Entertainment / Relatable | 20% | Reach and shares |
| Promotional / CTA | 10% | Conversions |
| Community / UGC | 10% | Engagement and loyalty |

## Caption Formulas

### The AIDA Formula
```
ATTENTION: [Hook that stops the scroll]
INTEREST: [What this is about + why it matters]
DESIRE: [What they'll get / the transformation]
ACTION: [One clear CTA — comment/save/DM/follow]
```

### The Story Formula
```
[Scene setting — before state]
[The turning point]
[The result — after state]
[What it means for them]
CTA: [What they should do next]
```

### The Value Drop
```
[Bold statement or surprising fact]
Here's what most people miss:
1. [Point 1]
2. [Point 2]
3. [Point 3]
[Synthesis — so what?]
CTA: Save this for later 🔖
```

## Reel Hook Ideas (First 3 Seconds)

The hook determines if someone watches or scrolls. Use these:

- "Stop doing [common thing] — here's why..."
- "I tried [thing] for 30 days. Here's what happened..."
- "The [result] nobody tells you about [topic]..."
- "POV: You finally figure out [thing]..."
- "[Number] things that [changed/helped/fixed] my [area]..."
- "What I wish someone told me about [topic]..."
- "Doing this one thing got me [result]..."

## Hashtag Strategy (2024)

Use 3–5 highly relevant hashtags:

| Type | Example | Use |
|------|---------|-----|
| Niche hashtag | #[yourniche]tips | Always |
| Content type | #[carouseltips] | When relevant |
| Community | #[ourcommunity] | Build belonging |
| Branded | #[yourbrand] | Optional |

Avoid: generic massive hashtags (#love, #instagood) — too competitive, no signal.

## Stories Strategy

**Daily story framework (5 slides max):**

1. **Opening question** — poll or question to boost views
2. **Value** — one tip, quote, or insight
3. **Personal** — behind scenes, humanizing moment
4. **Engagement** — quiz, emoji slider, or DM prompt
5. **CTA** — link, swipe up, or "DM me [keyword]"
