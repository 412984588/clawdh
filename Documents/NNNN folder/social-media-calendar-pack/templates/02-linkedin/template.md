# LinkedIn Content Calendar

A 30-day LinkedIn strategy to build professional authority, grow your network, and generate leads.

## Platform Overview

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Best posting frequency | 3–5x/week | Quality over quantity on LinkedIn |
| Peak posting times | Tue–Thu, 8–10am | Before the workday starts |
| Best content formats | Text posts, carousels, documents | Video growing fast in 2024 |
| Optimal post length | 1,200–1,500 characters | Use line breaks generously |
| Hook rule | First line must stop the scroll | LinkedIn shows 3 lines before "see more" |

| Content Type | Frequency | Goal |

| Educational / how-to | 1x/week | Drive saves and shares |

## 30-Day LinkedIn Calendar

### Week 1: Establish Your Angle

| Day | Format | Type | Post Idea |
|-----|--------|------|-----------|
| Mon | Text (long) | Opinion | "Why [conventional wisdom in your industry] is wrong" |
| Wed | Document/carousel | Educational | "[Number] things I learned in [X] years of [your field]" |
| Fri | Text (medium) | Story | "The worst [professional mistake] I made — and what it taught me" |

### Week 2: Share Your Expertise

| Day | Format | Type | Post Idea |
|-----|--------|------|-----------|
| Tue | Text + image | How-to | "The framework I use for [common challenge in your field]" |
| Thu | Document | Deep dive | "A [topic] guide that took me [X] years to learn" |
| Sat | Text | Reflection | "What nobody tells you about [your career path/industry]" |

### Week 3: Build Social Proof

| Day | Format | Type | Post Idea |
|-----|--------|------|-----------|
| Mon | Text | Case study | "How [I/my client] achieved [result] by doing [thing]" |
| Wed | Document | Resource | "[Number] [resources/frameworks/tools] every [your audience] needs" |
| Fri | Text | Milestone | "[Number] [milestone — followers/years/projects]. What I've learned." |

### Week 4: Convert Connections to Leads

| Day | Format | Type | Post Idea |
|-----|--------|------|-----------|

## LinkedIn Post Formula (The Hook-Body-CTA)

### Formula 1: The Contrarian Take
```
[Surprising or controversial statement that challenges common belief.]

[1 blank line]

Here's what most people miss:

[Body — 3–5 short paragraphs, each 1–3 lines]
[Use line breaks every 2–3 sentences]
[Build to a conclusion]

[1 blank line]

[CTA — question to drive comments, or what to do next]
```

### Formula 2: The Story Arc
```
[Vivid scene — "3 years ago, I was [situation]."]

[1 blank line]

[The problem / what went wrong]

[1 blank line]

[The turning point]

[1 blank line]

[The result / where you are now]

[1 blank line]

What this taught me:
→ [Lesson 1]
→ [Lesson 2]
→ [Lesson 3]

[1 blank line]

[Reflective question for comments]
```

### Formula 3: The List Post
```
[Bold hook line — "X things I wish I knew before [X]:"]

[1 blank line]

1/ [First point — 2–3 lines of explanation]

2/ [Second point]

3/ [Third point]

4/ [Fourth point]

5/ [Fifth point — save the best for last]

[1 blank line]

Which one resonates most? Comment below 👇
```

## Engagement Tactics

| Tactic | How | Expected Result |
|--------|-----|----------------|
| First-comment hook | Post an interesting first comment on your own post within first hour | +30–50% reach |
| Respond to every comment | Reply within 2 hours | Algorithm rewards activity |
| Engage on others' posts first | Comment on 5–10 posts before posting yours | Warms up the algorithm |
| Tag relevant people | Only when genuinely relevant | Extends reach |
| Newsletter | Send a monthly LinkedIn newsletter | Subscriber notifications = guaranteed reach |

## LinkedIn Profile Optimization Checklist

A great profile turns post readers into connections and clients:

- [ ] **Headline:** "[Your role] who helps [target audience] achieve [result]" — not just your job title
- [ ] **Banner:** Custom image showing your expertise or offer
- [ ] **About section:** First 3 lines are the hook — your story, who you help, your offer
- [ ] **Featured section:** Link to your best content, portfolio, or lead magnet
- [ ] **Experience:** Each role has 3–5 bullet points showing impact (numbers > responsibilities)
- [ ] **Custom URL:** linkedin.com/in/yourname
- [ ] **Creator Mode:** Turn on for newsletter + follower features
