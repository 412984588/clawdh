# Twitter/X Content Calendar

Build an audience on X with threads, hot takes, and consistent daily posting.

## Platform Overview

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Best posting frequency | 3–5 posts/day | X rewards volume more than other platforms |
| Peak posting times | 8–10am, 12–1pm, 6–8pm | Experiment with your audience |
| Best content types | Threads (authority), single tweets (reach), replies (growth) | Replies are underrated for growth |
| Optimal tweet length | 60–280 characters | Short tweets get more impressions |

## Reply Strategy (The Fastest Way to Grow)

Replying to larger accounts in your niche is the most efficient growth tactic on X.

**Daily reply routine (20 min):**
1. Find 5 posts from accounts in your niche with 10k+ followers
2. Reply with genuine, substantive insight (not "great post!" — that's invisible)
3. Your reply should be worth reading on its own — add a point, challenge an idea, share a relevant experience
4. Aim for top reply (most liked reply gets massive exposure)

**Good reply formula:**
```
[Acknowledge their point in one line]
[Add your perspective or experience]
[End with a question or insight]
```
