# YouTube Content Calendar

Build a YouTube channel that compounds over time — SEO, watch time, and subscriber growth.

## Platform Overview

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Best posting frequency | 1–2x/week | Quality > quantity; YouTube rewards consistency |
| Optimal video length | 8–15 min (main), 1–3 min (Shorts) | 8 min+ allows mid-roll ads |
| Best content types | Tutorials, listicles, case studies, vlogs | SEO-driven tutorials age best |
| Thumbnail CTR target | >5% | Biggest lever for growth after SEO |
| Title length | 50–70 characters | Front-load keywords |

## Video Structure Template

### Tutorial / How-To (8–12 min)
```
0:00 — Hook (30 sec): Start with the result or a bold claim
0:30 — Context (1 min): Why this matters, who it's for
1:30 — Overview (30 sec): "Here's what we'll cover..."
2:00 — Content (5–8 min): Step-by-step, one idea per segment
8–10 min — Summary: Recap the key takeaways
Final 30 sec — CTA: Subscribe, comment, watch next video
```

### Listicle / Top-N (8–12 min)
```
0:00 — Hook: "These [number] [things] will change how you [do topic]"
0:30 — Rapid intro (no long intros — subscribers skip them)
1:00 — #[N] → #1 format (save the best for last)
— Each point: 45–90 sec, specific example or visual
Final min — CTA + teaser for next video
```

### Opinion / Commentary (6–10 min)
```
0:00 — Strong opening claim
0:30 — Stakes: why this matters
1:00 — Your argument (3 supporting points)
8 min — Conclusion + call for comments
Final — "What do you think? Comment below"
```

## Title and Thumbnail Formula

### High-CTR Title Patterns
- "How to [Result] Without [Common Barrier]"
- "[Number] Mistakes [Your Audience] Makes (And How to Fix Them)"
- "Why [Conventional Advice] Is Wrong About [Topic]"
- "I Tried [Thing] for [Timeframe] — Here's What Happened"
- "The [Topic] Formula That [Result]"
- "[Year] Guide: [Topic] for [Audience]"

### Thumbnail Best Practices
| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Face | Include a face — faces get 38% more CTR |
| Text | Max 3–5 words, large and readable at small size |
| Contrast | High contrast colors — no dull backgrounds |
| Curiosity gap | Show the result without explaining how |
| Branding | Consistent style so subscribers recognize you instantly |

## YouTube SEO Checklist

For every video:
- [ ] Title includes primary keyword (front-loaded)
- [ ] Description: first 2 lines are keyword-rich (show above fold)
- [ ] Full description: 200+ words with timestamps and keywords
- [ ] 8–15 relevant tags (primary keyword first)
- [ ] Custom thumbnail with high-contrast design
- [ ] Pinned comment: CTA with chapter links
- [ ] End screen: 2 video recommendations + subscribe button (last 20 sec)
- [ ] Cards: 1–2 links to related videos at key moments

## YouTube Channel SEO Strategy

**Research workflow:**
1. Type your topic into YouTube search — note autocomplete suggestions (real queries)
2. Find a video with 50k–500k views in your niche that's 2+ years old (proven demand, rankable)
3. Create a better version: longer, more current, better thumbnails
4. Build playlists around topic clusters — playlist pages also rank in Google
