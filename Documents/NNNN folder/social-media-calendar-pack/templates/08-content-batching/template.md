# Content Batching System

Create a full month of social media content in one focused day. Stop creating daily — start batching weekly or monthly.

## Why Batching Works

| Approach | Time per piece | Mental switches | Quality |
|----------|---------------|-----------------|---------|
| Daily creation | 30–60 min | Constant | Inconsistent |
| Weekly batching | 15–25 min | Low | Consistent |
| Monthly batching | 10–20 min | Minimal | Highest |

**The core principle:** Creative flow takes 20 min to enter. Daily posting means you never stay in flow. Batching means you enter flow once and produce 4× as much.

---

## The Full Content Day (8-Hour Schedule)

### Pre-Work (Day Before — 30 min)
- [ ] Gather all source material: articles, notes, past ideas, analytics data
- [ ] Charge equipment, set up workspace
- [ ] Pull up your Content Ideas Bank (section below)
- [ ] Decide this month's theme/focus
- [ ] Block your calendar for the full Content Day

---

### Content Day Schedule

**8:00–9:00 — Ideation Sprint (60 min)**
- [ ] Review analytics from last month — what performed best?
- [ ] List 30–40 post ideas (don't filter — quantity first)
- [ ] Choose the best 25–30 (one per day of the month)
- [ ] Assign each idea to a format: Reel / Carousel / Single / Story / Thread

**9:00–11:00 — Writing Block (120 min)**
- [ ] Write all captions, scripts, and copy
- [ ] Group by platform (Instagram batch → LinkedIn batch → Twitter batch)
- [ ] Use the caption formulas from platform-specific templates
- [ ] Don't edit — write rough drafts for everything first

**11:00–11:15 — Break**

**11:15–13:00 — Visual Creation (105 min)**
- [ ] Open Canva (or your design tool) with your brand template
- [ ] Create all graphics, carousels, and quote cards
- [ ] Export all images to organized folder
- [ ] Resize for each platform if needed

**13:00–14:00 — Lunch Break**

**14:00–16:00 — Video Recording (120 min)**
- [ ] Record all Reels, TikToks, and YouTube Shorts scripts
- [ ] Film in batches by setup/location (don't change settings between shots)
- [ ] Record 10–15 short-form videos
- [ ] Record 1–2 long-form YouTube videos if applicable

**16:00–17:00 — Editing (60 min)**
- [ ] Basic edits: trim, captions, music
- [ ] Export all videos to organized folder
- [ ] Organize content library by platform and date

**17:00–18:00 — Scheduling (60 min)**
- [ ] Load content into your scheduler (Later, Buffer, Hootsuite, Metricool)
- [ ] Assign posting dates and times (use peak time data from platform templates)
- [ ] Write any missing captions or hashtag sets
- [ ] Final review — catch any errors or missing pieces

## Repurposing Matrix

One piece of content → multiple platforms:

| Original | → | Repurpose |
|----------|---|-----------|
| Long YouTube video | → | 3–5 Shorts / TikToks, 1 LinkedIn post, 3 tweets |
| LinkedIn article | → | 5 tweets, 1 Instagram carousel, 1 Pinterest infographic |
| Twitter thread | → | 1 LinkedIn post, 1 Instagram carousel, 1 email |
| Instagram Reel | → | TikTok, YouTube Short, Facebook Reel |
| Blog post | → | Pinterest pin, email newsletter, 5 social posts |
| Podcast episode | → | YouTube video, 3 clips, 10 quotes |

**Rule:** Every piece of content should appear on 3+ platforms.
