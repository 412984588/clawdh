# Social Media Analytics Tracker

Track performance across all platforms in one place. Monthly review drives 10× better results than posting blind.

## How to Use This Tracker

1. At the end of each week, fill in the weekly snapshot tables
2. At the end of each month, complete the monthly review
3. After 3 months, identify your top 20% content and double down
4. Use the "Improve" column to action one change per platform per month
