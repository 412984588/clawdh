# 90-Day Social Media Strategy

Stop winging it. A 90-day focused sprint beats a year of scattered posting.

## The 90-Day Framework

**Core principle:** Pick 1–2 platforms, dominate them, then expand. Most creators fail by spreading too thin across 5 platforms at once.

## Month 1: Build Your Content Engine (Days 1–30)

**Theme: Foundation and Consistency**

### Month 1 Priorities
1. Post every scheduled day without fail (consistency builds algorithm trust)
2. Find your best-performing content type (watch analytics closely in week 3)
3. Build your content production system (template library, batch schedule)
4. Engage deeply — respond to every comment and DM

### Week-by-Week Plan

| Week | Primary Focus | Daily Action | Goal |
|------|--------------|-------------|------|
| 1 | Setup and first 7 posts | 1 post/day + 30 min engagement | Establish rhythm |
| 2 | Find your hook formula | Test 3 different hook styles | Identify best-performing |
| 3 | Introduce your best content format | Double down on what Week 2 showed | 2× engagement vs Week 1 |
| 4 | First batch day | Create all Month 2 content | Reduce daily stress |

### Month 1 Content Calendar Template

| Week | Mon | Tue | Wed | Thu | Fri | Sat | Sun |
|------|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 1 | Post | Post | Engage | Post | Post | Engage | Rest |
| 2 | Post | Post | Engage | Post | Post | Engage | Rest |
| 3 | Post | Post | Engage | Post | Post | Engage | Rest |
| 4 | Batch Day | Post | Engage | Post | Post | Engage | Rest |

### Month 1 Success Criteria
- [ ] Posted on 20+ days out of 30
- [ ] Identified 1 content format that outperforms others
- [ ] Responded to all comments within 24 hours
- [ ] Gained [your target] new followers
- [ ] Completed analytics review (Week 4)
