# Resume Template

A modern, ATS-friendly resume structure. Fill in your details, customize per role, and submit confidently.

## Resume Structure (Recommended Order)

### 1. Header

```
[YOUR FULL NAME]
[City, State] | [Phone] | [Email] | [LinkedIn URL] | [Portfolio URL (optional)]
```

### 2. Professional Summary (2–3 sentences)

```
[Title/Role] with [X] years of experience in [key skill areas]. Proven track
record of [measurable achievement]. Seeking [target role] at [type of company]
to [value you'll bring].
```

**Examples:**

For experienced professionals:
> Marketing Manager with 7 years of experience driving B2B growth. Led campaigns that generated $2.4M in pipeline and increased conversion rates by 34%. Seeking a Senior Marketing role at a growth-stage SaaS company.

For career changers:
> Former teacher transitioning to UX Design with a Google UX Design Certificate and 3 portfolio projects. Brings strong research skills, empathy-driven design thinking, and experience presenting to diverse audiences.

For recent graduates:
> Computer Science graduate from [University] with internship experience at [Company]. Built 3 full-stack applications and contributed to an open-source project with 500+ stars. Seeking entry-level software engineering roles.

---

### 3. Work Experience

**Format for each role:**
```
[JOB TITLE] | [COMPANY NAME] | [City, State]
[Start Date] – [End Date or Present]

- [Action verb] [what you did] [measurable result]
- [Action verb] [what you did] [measurable result]
- [Action verb] [what you did] [measurable result]
```

**Power verbs by category:**

| Category | Verbs |
|----------|-------|
| Leadership | Led, Managed, Directed, Oversaw, Coordinated, Mentored |
| Achievement | Achieved, Exceeded, Delivered, Completed, Surpassed |
| Creation | Built, Designed, Developed, Created, Launched, Established |
| Improvement | Improved, Optimized, Streamlined, Reduced, Increased, Enhanced |
| Analysis | Analyzed, Evaluated, Assessed, Identified, Researched, Measured |
| Communication | Presented, Negotiated, Collaborated, Facilitated, Trained |

**Bullet point formula:**
```
[Power verb] + [what you did] + [for whom/what] + [result with numbers]

Example: "Reduced customer churn by 18% by implementing a proactive outreach
program for at-risk accounts, saving $340K in annual recurring revenue."
```

## ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Rules

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| Use standard section headers | ATS looks for "Experience", "Education", "Skills" — not creative alternatives |
| No tables, columns, or graphics | ATS can't parse complex layouts |
| Use standard fonts | Arial, Calibri, Times New Roman, Helvetica |
| Save as PDF (unless told otherwise) | Preserves formatting |
| Include keywords from the job description | ATS scores resumes by keyword match |
| No headers or footers | Some ATS systems skip header/footer content |

## Resume Customization Checklist (Per Application)

Before submitting to each job:

- [ ] Read the full job description
- [ ] Highlight 5–8 key skills/requirements mentioned
- [ ] Mirror those keywords in your resume (naturally)
- [ ] Adjust your professional summary for this specific role
- [ ] Reorder bullet points to prioritize most relevant experience
- [ ] Remove irrelevant experience if resume exceeds 1 page
- [ ] Proofread for typos (read backwards to catch errors)
- [ ] Save as "[YourName]_Resume_[CompanyName].pdf"

---

## Resume Length Guide

| Experience Level | Pages | Notes |
|-----------------|-------|-------|
| 0–5 years | 1 page | Strict — no exceptions |
| 5–15 years | 1–2 pages | Only 2 if genuinely needed |
| 15+ years | 2 pages | Trim old/irrelevant roles |
| Academic / research | 2+ pages | CV format acceptable |
