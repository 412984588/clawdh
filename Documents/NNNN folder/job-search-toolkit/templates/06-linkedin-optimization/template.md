# LinkedIn Profile Optimization

93% of recruiters use LinkedIn to find candidates. Make sure they find you.

## Profile Completion Checklist

### Must-Have (Non-Negotiable)
- [ ] Professional headshot (face fills 60% of frame, good lighting, neutral background)
- [ ] Custom headline (NOT "Software Engineer at Company" — see below)
- [ ] About/Summary section (see template below)
- [ ] Current role with bullet points
- [ ] At least 2 past roles
- [ ] Education
- [ ] 5+ skills listed
- [ ] Custom URL (linkedin.com/in/yourname)
- [ ] Location set correctly
- [ ] Open to Work badge (visible to recruiters only)

### High Impact
- [ ] Banner image (branded or industry-related)
- [ ] Featured section (portfolio, articles, presentations)
- [ ] Recommendations (3+ given and received)
- [ ] 500+ connections
- [ ] Creator mode ON (if posting content)

---

## Headline Formula (120 Characters)

Your headline appears in search results, connection requests, and comments. Make it count.

**Formula:** `[Role] | [Key skill] | [Value you deliver]`

**Examples:**
- `Marketing Manager | B2B SaaS Growth | Drove $2.4M Pipeline at [Company]`
- `Full-Stack Developer | React + Node.js | Building Products Users Love`
- `Data Analyst | SQL, Python, Tableau | Turning Data into Decisions`
- `Product Manager | AI/ML Products | 0→1 at Series B Startups`
- `Career Changer → UX Designer | User Research | Google UX Certified`

## LinkedIn SEO: Keywords That Get You Found

Recruiters search LinkedIn like Google. Include these keywords:

### Where to Place Keywords

3. **Job titles** — high weight
4. **Skills section** — medium weight
5. **Job descriptions** — medium weight

### How to Find the Right Keywords
1. Search 10 job postings for your target role

## Weekly LinkedIn Activity (Job Seekers)

| Day | Activity | Time |
|-----|---------|------|
| Mon | Post content (insight, lesson, or industry take) | 15 min |
| Tue | Comment on 5 posts from target companies/roles | 10 min |
| Wed | Send 5 connection requests with personalized notes | 10 min |
| Thu | Post content or engage with others' posts | 15 min |
| Fri | Review who viewed your profile — connect with relevant ones | 10 min |

**Weekly total: ~60 min**

## Connection Request Templates

**To a recruiter:**
> Hi [Name], I noticed you recruit for [type of role] at [Company]. I'm a [your title] with [X] years in [field] and I'm exploring opportunities. Would love to connect.

**To someone at a target company:**
> Hi [Name], I'm researching [Company] as a potential next step in my career. I'd love to learn about your experience there. Would you be open to a brief chat?

**After meeting someone:**
> Great meeting you at [event/conversation]! Enjoyed our discussion about [topic]. Let's stay connected.
