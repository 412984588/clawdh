# Interview Preparation Guide

Prepare for any interview in 60 minutes. Research, practice, and walk in confident.

## Pre-Interview Research (20 min)

### Company Research Checklist

- [ ] What does the company do? (1 sentence)
- [ ] What's their revenue model? (How do they make money?)
- [ ] Recent news (last 3 months — press releases, funding, launches)
- [ ] Company values / mission (from About page)
- [ ] Who are their competitors?
- [ ] Who's interviewing you? (LinkedIn — their background and interests)
- [ ] What's the team structure?
- [ ] Glassdoor reviews — any consistent themes?

## The STAR Method (For Behavioral Questions)

Every behavioral answer follows this structure:

| Component | What to Say | Time |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| **S**ituation | Set the scene — where, when, what was happening | 15 sec |
| **T**ask | What was your specific responsibility | 10 sec |
| **A**ction | What YOU did (not the team) — be specific | 45 sec |
| **R**esult | Quantified outcome — numbers, percentages, impact | 15 sec |

**Total: ~90 seconds per answer**

## Top 20 Interview Questions + Answer Frameworks

| # | Question | Framework |
|---|---------|----------|
| 1 | Tell me about yourself | 2 min: Past → Present → Future (why this role) |
| 2 | Why do you want this job? | Company research + your skills + career alignment |
| 3 | What's your greatest strength? | Strength + STAR example proving it |
| 4 | What's your greatest weakness? | Real weakness + what you're doing to improve |
| 5 | Tell me about a challenge you overcame | STAR Story #2 |
| 6 | Where do you see yourself in 5 years? | Growth in this field, not specific titles |
| 7 | Why are you leaving your current role? | Positive framing — what you're moving toward |
| 8 | Tell me about a time you led a team | STAR Story #3 |
| 9 | How do you handle conflict? | STAR Story #6 |
| 10 | What makes you unique? | 2–3 differentiators with brief examples |
| 11 | Describe a time you failed | STAR Story #4 + emphasis on learning |
| 12 | How do you prioritize work? | Your system + example |
| 13 | Tell me about a tight deadline | STAR Story #5 |
| 14 | What do you know about our company? | Research from above |
| 15 | Why should we hire you? | 3 reasons tied to job requirements |
| 16 | Describe your work style | Honest + adaptable to their culture |
| 17 | How do you handle feedback? | Welcome it + STAR example |
| 18 | Tell me about a time you disagreed with a manager | STAR — respectful disagreement + outcome |
| 19 | What questions do you have for us? | See "Questions to Ask" below |
| 20 | What are your salary expectations? | See Salary Negotiation (template 05) |

## Questions to Ask the Interviewer (Pick 3–5)

| Category | Question |
|----------|---------|
| Role | "What does a typical day/week look like in this role?" |
| Success | "How will success be measured in the first 6 months?" |
| Team | "Can you tell me about the team I'd be working with?" |
| Growth | "What growth opportunities exist for this role?" |
| Culture | "What do you personally enjoy most about working here?" |
| Challenges | "What's the biggest challenge the team is facing right now?" |
| Process | "What are the next steps in the interview process?" |

**Never ask about salary, PTO, or benefits in the first interview.** Save for later stages or after you receive an offer.
