# Networking Scripts & Templates

80% of jobs are filled through networking. These scripts make it less awkward.

## The Informational Interview

### How to Request One

**Email/LinkedIn template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about [their role/company]

Hi [Name],

I'm [your name], a [your title] exploring opportunities in [field/industry].
I came across your profile on LinkedIn and was impressed by your work at
[Company], particularly [specific thing].

Would you have 20 minutes for a brief chat? I'd love to learn about your
experience in [specific area]. Happy to work around your schedule.

No pressure at all — I appreciate your time either way.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### The 20-Minute Informational Interview Script

**Opening (2 min):**
"Thanks so much for taking the time. I'm [name], currently [what you do]. I'm exploring [career direction] and your background in [area] is really interesting to me."

**Questions to Ask (15 min — pick 5):**

| Category | Question |
|----------|---------|
| Their path | "How did you get into [their role/field]?" |
| Day-to-day | "What does a typical week look like in your role?" |
| Skills | "What skills have been most valuable in your career?" |
| Industry | "What trends are you seeing in [industry] right now?" |
| Advice | "If you were starting your career today, what would you do differently?" |
| Company | "What's the culture like at [Company]?" |
| Challenges | "What's the most challenging part of your role?" |
| Hiring | "What do you look for when hiring for your team?" |
| Transition | "Any advice for someone transitioning from [your field] to [their field]?" |
| Resources | "Are there any books, courses, or communities you'd recommend?" |

**Closing (3 min):**
"This has been incredibly helpful. Two last things — is there anyone else you'd recommend I talk to? And is there anything I can do to help you?"

### Follow-Up (Send Within 24 Hours)

```
Subject: Thank you, [Name]!

Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to chat today. Your insights about
[specific thing they mentioned] were really valuable — especially
[one specific takeaway].

I'm going to [action you're taking based on their advice]. If [person
they recommended] is open to a chat, I'd appreciate the intro.

Thanks again. I'll keep you posted on how things go.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Cold Outreach Templates

### To Someone at a Target Company

```
Subject: Fellow [industry/school/interest] person — quick intro

Hi [Name],

I'm [your name], a [title] with [X] years in [field]. I've been following
[Company] since [specific event — launch, article, product], and I'm
really interested in the [specific team/area].

I noticed [something specific about their work — article, project, post].
Would you be open to a 15-minute chat about your experience there?

Totally understand if you're busy. Thanks either way.

[Your Name]
```

### To a Recruiter

```
Subject: [Your Title] — interested in opportunities at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I'm a [title] with [X] years of experience in [key skills]. I'm actively
exploring roles in [target area] and [Company] is high on my list because
[specific reason].

My background includes [1-2 relevant achievements with numbers]. I've
attached my resume and would love to be considered for any relevant
openings.

Happy to chat whenever works for you.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Networking Event Scripts

### Introduction (30 Seconds)

```
"Hi, I'm [Name]. I'm a [title] focused on [what you do]. I'm here because
[why you came to this event]. What brought you here?"
```

### Follow-Up After Meeting Someone

```
Subject: Great meeting you at [Event]!

Hi [Name],

It was great chatting at [event] about [topic you discussed]. I really
liked your take on [specific thing].

[If relevant: I found that resource we talked about — here's the link.]

Would love to stay in touch. Let's connect on LinkedIn or grab coffee
sometime.

[Your Name]
```

## Referral Request Templates

### Asking Someone You Know

```
Hi [Name],

I hope you're doing well! I noticed [Company] has an opening for [Role]
and I think it could be a great fit for my background in [relevant skills].

Would you be comfortable referring me? I've attached my resume and I'm
happy to send you a short blurb you can forward to the hiring manager.

Totally understand if it's not the right time. I really appreciate you
even considering it.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### The Blurb to Make It Easy for Them

```
"[Your Name] is a [title] with [X] years of experience in [key areas].
They [1 achievement with numbers]. They're interested in the [Role]
position and I think they'd be a strong fit because [1 specific reason]."
```
