# Follow-Up Email Templates

The follow-up is where most job seekers drop the ball. These templates make it automatic.

## After Applying (1 Week, No Response)

```
Subject: Following up — [Job Title] application

Hi [Name / Hiring Team],

I applied for the [Job Title] position on [date] and wanted to follow up
to express my continued interest.

I'm particularly excited about [specific aspect of the role or company],
and I believe my experience in [relevant skill] — including [brief
achievement] — would be a strong fit.

I've attached my resume for your convenience. Is there any additional
information I can provide?

Thank you for your time.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

---

## After a Phone Screen (Within 24 Hours)

```
Subject: Thank you — [Job Title] phone screen

Hi [Name],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today about the [Job Title]
role. I enjoyed learning about [specific thing discussed — team structure,
current projects, company direction].

Our conversation reinforced my excitement about this opportunity,
particularly [specific aspect]. My experience in [relevant area] aligns
well with what you described.

I'm looking forward to the next steps. Please let me know if there's
anything else you need from me.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

## After an In-Person / Video Interview (Within 24 Hours)

```
Subject: Thank you — [Job Title] interview

Dear [Interviewer Name],

Thank you for the interview today. I really enjoyed meeting the team and
learning more about [specific project or challenge they mentioned].

I was especially excited to hear about [specific thing from the conversation].
My experience with [relevant skill or project] has prepared me well for
exactly this type of work.

One thing I'd like to add that we didn't get to discuss: [brief additional
point that strengthens your candidacy].

I'm very enthusiastic about this role and look forward to hearing about
next steps.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
```

**If you interviewed with multiple people:**
Send a unique thank-you to each person. Reference something specific from YOUR conversation with THEM.

---

## After a Final Round (Within 24 Hours)

```
Subject: Thank you — final round for [Job Title]

Dear [Name],

Thank you for the opportunity to meet with the team during the final round.
After spending [X hours] with [names], I'm even more confident that this
role is the right fit.

What stood out most was [specific cultural/team/project observation].
I'm excited about the opportunity to [specific contribution you'd make].

I'm ready to move forward and look forward to your decision. Please don't
hesitate to reach out if you need any additional information.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Checking In (When They Said "We'll Get Back to You" and Didn't)

### 1 Week After Expected Response

```
Subject: Checking in — [Job Title] at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on our conversation from [date]. I remain very
interested in the [Job Title] role and would love to know if there are
any updates on the timeline.

I'm happy to provide any additional information that would be helpful.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
```

### 2 Weeks After Expected Response

```
Subject: Re: [Job Title] — still very interested

Hi [Name],

I hope you're doing well. I wanted to check in one more time regarding
the [Job Title] position. I understand hiring timelines can shift, and
I'm happy to be patient.

This role remains my top choice, and I'd appreciate any update you can
share, even if it's just on timing.

Thanks so much,
[Your Name]
```

**After 3 follow-ups with no response: move on. They're not interested or the role is frozen.**

## After Receiving a Rejection

```
Subject: Thank you — [Job Title]

Hi [Name],

Thank you for letting me know. While I'm disappointed, I appreciate
the opportunity to interview and learn about [Company].

If you're open to it, I'd value any feedback on my interview performance
so I can continue to improve.

I'd also love to stay connected — if a similar role opens up in the
future, I'd be very interested.

Thanks again for your time throughout the process.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

---

## After Accepting an Offer

```
Subject: Excited to join [Company]!

Dear [Name],

I'm thrilled to officially accept the [Job Title] offer. Thank you for
this incredible opportunity — I can't wait to join the team on [start date].

Please let me know if there's anything I should do to prepare before
my first day, or any paperwork I should complete in advance.

Looking forward to it!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

---

## Follow-Up Timing Cheat Sheet

| Situation | When to Follow Up |
|-----------|------------------|
| After applying (no response) | 1 week |
| After phone screen | Same day or next morning |
| After in-person interview | Same day or next morning |
| After final round | Same day |
| "We'll get back to you by Friday" | Following Monday |
| After rejection | Same day |
| After accepting | Same day |
| Networking follow-up | Within 24 hours |
