# FAQ — Job Search Toolkit

**Is this for a specific industry?**
No. The templates work for any industry — tech, finance, marketing, healthcare, education, etc. The frameworks (STAR method, salary negotiation, etc.) are universal.

**I'm a recent graduate with no experience. Will this work?**
Yes. The resume template includes examples for recent graduates. The interview prep includes frameworks that work even without professional experience (use school projects, volunteer work, internships).

**Do I get actual resume/cover letter text I can copy?**
You get fill-in-the-blank frameworks with examples. The templates show you exactly what to write, where — but you fill in YOUR details. This is better than copy-paste because it's personalized.

**What format are the files?**
Markdown (.md) — open in Notion, Obsidian, Google Docs, or any text editor.

**Is the salary negotiation advice real?**
Yes. Based on established negotiation frameworks. The average negotiated offer is $5,000–$10,000 higher than the first offer. The templates include word-for-word scripts.

**Can I use this toolkit more than once?**
Absolutely. The templates are reusable for every job search. Update your STAR stories, customize your resume, and track applications each time.
