# FAQ — Personal Finance Dashboard

**Is this a spreadsheet or an app?**
Neither. These are Markdown (.md) templates — plain text files you edit directly. Import into Notion, Obsidian, or any text editor.

**Do I need to know anything about finance to use these?**
No. Each template explains the concepts (50/30/20 budget, debt snowball vs. avalanche, asset allocation by age) and gives you fill-in-the-blank tables.

**Will this work for freelancers/self-employed?**
Yes. The Tax Prep template includes a full self-employment section with business expense categories and quarterly estimated tax tracking.

**Is this US-specific?**
The tax template uses US tax forms and limits. All other templates (budget, savings, debt, investing) work regardless of country.

**How is this different from Mint or YNAB?**
Those are apps that auto-import transactions. This is a manual system you control — no bank connections, no subscriptions, no company shutting down. Many people prefer manual tracking because it forces awareness of every dollar.

**Can I customize the templates?**
Yes — they're your files. Change categories, add rows, modify formulas to match your situation.
