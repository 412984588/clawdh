# Gumroad Listing — Personal Finance Dashboard

## Product Name
Personal Finance Dashboard — Budget, Track, Save, Invest

## Tagline
A complete personal finance system in Markdown. Budget, track expenses, pay off debt, build savings, and review annually.

## Price Points
- Starter: $19 (5 templates)
- Pro: $39 (all 10 templates)

## Description

Most finance apps are too complex or too simple. This is the system in between: structured enough to work, flexible enough to customize.

**10 fill-in templates:**
- Monthly Budget (50/30/20 framework with sinking funds)
- Expense Tracker (daily log with spending insights)
- Savings Goals (multi-goal tracker with milestones)
- Debt Payoff (Avalanche and Snowball calculators)
- Net Worth (monthly tracker with annual comparison)
- Investment Tracker (allocation, holdings, performance)
- Tax Prep (income, deductions, credits, self-employment)
- Emergency Fund (calculator, savings log, account guide)
- Subscription Audit (find hidden charges, save $100+/month)
- Annual Review (year-end retrospective with next-year goals)

Markdown format — paste into Notion, Obsidian, or any editor.
