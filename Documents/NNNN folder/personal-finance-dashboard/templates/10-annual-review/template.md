## Annual Checklist

### Insurance Review
- [ ] Health insurance — best plan for next year?
- [ ] Car insurance — shop for better rate
- [ ] Renters/homeowners insurance — coverage adequate?
- [ ] Life insurance — coverage matches current needs?
- [ ] Umbrella policy — needed at your net worth level?

### Retirement
- [ ] Maximize 401(k) employer match
- [ ] Increase retirement contribution by 1%
- [ ] Review investment allocation vs. age target
- [ ] Check IRA contribution eligibility

### Tax Planning
- [ ] Estimate tax liability for next year
- [ ] Adjust W-4 if refund was >$1,000 or owed >$500
- [ ] Max out tax-advantaged accounts (401k, IRA, HSA)
- [ ] Consider Roth conversion if in low-income year
- [ ] Harvest tax losses in taxable accounts

### Credit
- [ ] Check credit score (annualcreditreport.com — free)
- [ ] Dispute any errors
- [ ] Review credit card benefits — are you maximizing rewards?

### Estate
- [ ] Beneficiaries up to date on all accounts?
- [ ] Will / trust — do you have one? Does it need updating?
- [ ] Power of attorney designated?
