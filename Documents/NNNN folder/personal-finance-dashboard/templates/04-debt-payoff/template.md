## Debt Payoff Calculator

```
Months to payoff = -log(1 - (Rate × Balance / Payment)) / log(1 + Rate)
where Rate = Annual APR / 12 / 100

Simplified estimate:
Months ≈ Balance / (Monthly Payment - (Balance × APR / 12 / 100))
```
