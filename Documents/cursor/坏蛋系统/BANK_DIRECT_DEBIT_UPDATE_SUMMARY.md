- `automatic withdrawal` - 自动提取
- `bank draft` - 银行汇票
- `electronic funds transfer` - 电子资金转账
- `eft` - 电子资金转账缩写

#### 客户授权和执行
- `pull payment` - 拉取付款
- `pull funds` - 拉取资金
- `ach debit` - ACH借记
- `payment authorization` - 付款授权
- `customer authorization` - 客户授权
- `scheduled payments` - 定期付款
- `recurring payments` - 循环付款
