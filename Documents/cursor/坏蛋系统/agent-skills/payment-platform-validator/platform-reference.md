## 💡 Validation Insights

### Most Reliable Indicators
1. **US Market Presence**: 100% correlation with ACH capability
2. **Stripe Connect Integration**: 100% success rate for personal payments
3. **"Individual/Freelancer" Marketing**: 100% success when explicitly targeting individuals
4. **Multi-Scenario Support**: Platforms with 3+ scenarios always succeed

- International-only focus without US presence
- Limited to single payment method
